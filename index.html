Choose Your Project Track!

At Garuda Hacks 2.0, we strive to solve Indonesia's most pressing issues.
This year, Garuda Hacks 2.0 will focus on tackling:

Small Businesses

Entrepreneurialism is at the heart of Indonesian society. 97% of the Indonesian workforce work in Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs), making them the key engine to economic growth. Starting a business is never easy, but there is so much promise for tech to help mom and pop shops.

How do we:

  • Simplify certain processes for businessowners so that they can focus on running their business?
  • Reduce costs and improve profit margins
  • Open new markets for businessowners
  • Better improve customer and businessowner interaction

Sustainability

In today's world it is evident that the climate crisis is affecting our planet. Indonesia—with its rich oceans and lush forests—is at the forefront of this struggle. Yet, the journey to save our communities shouldn’t be a lonely walk, but a unified effort that empowers our own communities to take action.

How do we:

  • Educate people about sustainability and best practices
  • Create grassroots initiatives to keep our natural resources clean
  • Implement technology to detect real-time changes to our environment
  • Create businesses that not only make money, but look after the environment

Opportunity

With rapid economic growth, some stand to benefit more than others. We believe that regardless of where you come from, you shouldn’t be constrained in finding opportunities. Now with tech, the playing field in mitigating inequality has changed.

How do we:

  • Provide programs that equip people hoping to enter the workforce with transferrable skills
  • Create accessibility and inclusivity for people in education, jobs, or finding a living space
  • Leverage Indonesian connectivity and infrastructure to connect people to opportunities
  • Increase our economic prosperity through technology

Frequently Asked Questions

We know you have a lot of questions, so check below for some of the most common questions we’ve received.

For further questions, you may also contact us at [email protected]

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is a programming competition in which competitors use technology to build innovative projects within 36 hours. There will be opportunities to win cool prizes, learn from our key-note speakers, and network with our sponsors.

Where will it be hosted?

Garuda Hacks 2.0 will be hosted on November 19-21, 2021, completely virtually. All you need is to create a Slack account to communicate with our community.

Who can participate?

Everyone can participate! We encourage anyone who is interested in learning more about Hackathons, programming, and the tech industry to join Garuda Hacks 2.0!

Do I have to know how to code to join?

Nope! You can learn along the way or team up with experienced programmers. Skills such as designing, business planning, and etc are all going to impact the projects in various ways!

Are there any rules?

Attendees must abide by the Major League Hacking (MLH) Code of Conduct. We want to respect each other and keep Garuda Hacks 2.0 a safe environment for all participants.

What if I don’t have a team?

You can apply as an individual or as a team (up to 4 participants max). If in any case, you are looking for team members, you can form teams with other participating hackers at the beginning of our event. Don’t worry, we will help you pair up with other hackers!

How much will Garuda Hacks 2.0 cost me?

Nothing at all!

How do we submit our projects?

You must submit your project’s video demonstration through Devpost. Additionally, it is optional for you to submit your project’s live website too.

What can I make?

We welcome all software and hardware hacks that align with our tracks.
